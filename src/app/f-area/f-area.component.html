The Best of Adventure Activities in India

We live in a wonderful world that is full of beauty, charm and adventure. There is no end to the adventures that we can have if only we seek them with our eyes open.

HOT-AIR BALLOONING
Rajasthan

Hot air balloon rides in the 'Pink City' Jaipur, is one of the most favorite pastimes for tourists from India and abroad.

TREKKING
Jammmu & Kashmir

Trekking might sound like a very common and relatively easy activity for adventure in India. trekking can be the one of the most thrilling activities.

CLIFF JUMPING
Rishikesh

Cliff Jumping is a popular adventure activity in the rugged terrains of Shivpuri. This place is thronged by adventure buffs all through the year.

PARA GLIDING
Manali

Paragliding is the tailor-made activity for you! Whether it is the lush greenery of the valleys that you would like to gaze.

RAFTING
Uttarakhand

Rafting is the most common adventure activity of India. Every adventure enthusiast starts off by setting the foot in the waters for a swift rafting experience.

WATERFALL RAPELLING
Kerela

Rappelling is an adventure sport that allows you to descend down from a cliff using a rope.As the name suggests, is descending down from a waterfall using the same technique.
